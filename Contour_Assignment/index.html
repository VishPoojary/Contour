How to Get There

Our Glen Waverley Office is within walking distance from major transport hubs including Glen Waverley Railway Station and local bus routes. Its location next to Springvale Road and proximity to High Street Road provides accessibility to major Eastern Suburbs.

Here are a few ways you can reach our office:

  • 2-minute walk from Glen Waverley Railway Station (Glen Waverley Line).
  • 2-minute walk from nearby Route 623/734/736/737/742/753/754/850/885/902/967 bus stops (Glen Waverley Bus Loop).
  • 15-minute bus ride from Monash University.
